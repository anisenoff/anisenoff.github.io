Computer Science

I'm incredibly lucky to be doing cs research with a few different professors.

This summer I am interning at the Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy in the Human Factors in Security and Privacy Group with Dr.Yasemin Acar and TeamUSEC at Leibniz University Hannover.

I've been a part of the SUPERgroup for about two years, where I work with Professor Blase Ur. My current projects are looking at breakage in browsers due to tracking protection, investigating the prevalence of password reuse here at UChicago, and trying to understand pin extension strategies.

Lastly, I'm looking into how people understand and interact with encrypted DNS settings in browsers and mobile devices with Professor Nick Feamster.
